About Us

We are a family-owned food truck business that specializes in traditional Brazilian pastels. Our pastels are made with the freshest ingredients and authentic Brazilian flavors. We have been serving our community for over 10 years and have a loyal customer base that keeps coming back for more.


Our Mission

Our mission is to provide our customers with a unique and delicious experience that transports them to Brazil with every bite. We strive to maintain the highest standards of quality, freshness, and authenticity in everything we do.
